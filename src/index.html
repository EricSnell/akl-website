Who We Are

Amaru-Khan Logistics (AKL) is a woman owned, all-ground transportation company that provides Transportation, Delivery Services, Courier Services, and Logistics Management. Headquartered in Northeast Ohio, ILF provides transport services across the United States and abroad. We are a certified 8(a), HUBZone, WOSB, MBE, DBE, EDGE and EDWOSB firm, servicing corporate, state and government agencies.

Our Mission

AKL’s mission is to provide world-class, certified transportation services. The honor of being a safe, friendly and reliable non-emergency transportation service that transports patients, medical supplies and correspondence is at our core. As a trusted partner, we also orchestrate and manage strategic and logistical transportation plans. We believe a kind, caring and compassionate person’s words can go a long way, inspiring one day at a time. We are committed to providing positive customer service to exceed our clients’ and customers’ expectations in an ethical manner.
