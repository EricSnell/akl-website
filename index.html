Who We Are

Amaru-Khan Logistics Company (AKL) is a woman owned, all-ground transportation company that provides Transportation, Delivery Services, Courier Services, and Logistics Management. Headquartered in Northeast Ohio, AKL provides transport services across the United States and abroad. We are a certified 8(a), HUBZone, WOSB, MBE, DBE, EDGE and EDWOSB firm, servicing corporate, state and government agencies.
